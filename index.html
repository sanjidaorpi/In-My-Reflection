WHERE AM I?

Socrates famously said, â€œThe unexamined life is not worth living." This interactive experience is a space for you to examine your life and engage in meaningful self reflection by answering a series of questions. Dive deep into better knowing yourself.

HOW TO:

Scroll below to get started. Click on the start button and enable camera and microphone access. There will be four questions, each displayed at the top of the frame. When you are ready to answer a question, click on the microphone button at the bottom of the frame, verbally answer the question and click the microphone button again to switch it off. Your answer will be recorded and visibily appear on the screen along with your reflection. Take your time to think about your answer, you only have one chance. Once you are satisfied with the answer, click on the arrow button to the right to move on to the next question. Once you complete the four questions, you will see a reflection of all of your answers, where you will be given an option to save a png of them.
